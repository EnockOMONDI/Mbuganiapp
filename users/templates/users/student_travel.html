About Our Partnership

Mbugani Luxe Adventures is a company that offers a full spectrum of tours and travel solutions from anywhere across the globe. Our partnership offers a range of services including air ticketing, hotel reservation, tours, M.I.C.E, corporate travel and students travel which encompass sports travel and in this case world scholars' program.

World Scholars Cup

This is a prestigious international academic competition designed to nurture and challenge young minds through critical thinking, global debate, collaborative writing and team challenges. The program promotes cultural exchange, academic excellence, and leadership development in a globally competitive environment by bringing together students from across the globe.

Participating students engage in diverse topics ranging from science and literature to politics, society and the arts. This experience fundamentally enhances academic performance, builds confidence, communication skills and global awareness of students.

Stages and Mbugani Luxe Adventures Partnership

Comprehensive support throughout your World Scholars Cup journey

1

Registration & Training

School-Level Mobilization And Registration

Program Activities

  • Students register for the WSC (Junior or Senior Division)
  • Schools organize internal training and club activities
  • Registration for regional rounds begins

Mbugani Luxe Adventures Partnership

  • Organize kick-off meetings and information seminars for students and parents (MICE)
  • Assist with logistics for training retreats or scholar camps - transport, meals, venues
  • Offer customized information kits and orientation travel packages
2

Regional Round

Takes place in major cities like Nairobi

Competition Activities

  • Team Debates
  • Collaborative Writing
  • Scholar's Bowl
  • Scholar's Challenge

Mbugani Luxe Adventures Partnership

  • Provide transport solutions for students within and outside Nairobi
  • Arrange affordable accommodation for out-of-town students and staff
  • Support with event logistics to schools hosting the round (venue setup, refreshments, branding)
  • Offer hospitality packages and concierge services for chaperones
3

Global Round

Hosted in cities like Doha, Bangkok, Kuala Lumpur

International Competition

  • Qualified teams travel abroad for global competition
  • Cultural events like the Scholar's Ball
  • Cultural Fair participation
  • International networking opportunities

Mbugani Luxe Adventures Partnership

  • Full air ticketing solutions and visa processing support
  • Coordinate group travel bookings with cost-saving group rates
  • Book international accommodation close to the event venue
  • Offer tour add-ons (city sightseeing)
  • Coordinate travel insurance, airport transfers and emergency support abroad
4

Tournament of Champions

Held at Yale University, USA

Elite Competition

  • Top global teams qualify for the final round at Yale
  • Competitive academic experiences
  • Cultural experiences at prestigious university
  • Networking with global academic elite

Mbugani Luxe Adventures Partnership

  • Handle US visa application guidance and appointment scheduling
  • Organize multi-leg travel itineraries with parental consent procedures
  • Secure premium student accommodation and university tour extensions
  • Offer travel coordination for parents or guardians accompanying students
  • Arrange post-event leisure trips (New York, Boston, or Washington D.C. excursions)
